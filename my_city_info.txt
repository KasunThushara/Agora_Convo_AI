Central City Mall - Complete Guide

Overview
Welcome to Central City Mall, one of the most vibrant and diverse shopping centers in the region. As your tour guide today, I will help you explore everything this mall has to offer—from food courts and entertainment zones to conference facilities and easy transportation access. Whether you are here to shop, relax, or attend an event, Central City Mall provides a complete experience for visitors of all ages.

Ground Floor

Main Entrance
The main entrance welcomes visitors with wide automatic doors and accessible ramps. Security and information desks are located immediately inside.

Coffee Breeze Café
Location: Ground floor, to the left of the main entrance
Specialty: Premium coffee, pastries, and light breakfast items
Hours: 7:00 AM - 10:00 PM daily
The café offers a cozy seating area perfect for meetings or casual relaxation.

Supermarket
A full-service supermarket is located on the ground floor. It provides groceries, fresh produce, household items, and daily essentials. The supermarket connects to a hallway leading to the subway entrance.

Washrooms
Washrooms are located near the escalators on the ground floor. They are well-maintained, accessible, and include facilities for people with disabilities.

Second Floor

Food Court
The second floor hosts the main food court with diverse dining options. Seating areas accommodate large groups and families.

Dragon Wok Restaurant
Cuisine: Chinese dishes
Specialties: Fried rice, noodles, dim sum, and authentic Chinese cuisine
Location: Food court, second floor
Hours: 10:00 AM - 9:00 PM

Ceylon Spice Kitchen
Cuisine: Sri Lankan food
Specialties: Rice and curry, kottu roti, hoppers, and traditional Sri Lankan dishes
Location: Near the main food court area, second floor
Hours: 10:00 AM - 9:00 PM

Other Dining Options
The food court also includes pizza, burgers, Indian cuisine, and dessert shops.

Washrooms
Main washroom facilities are located near the escalators on the second floor. They are larger and include family restrooms.

Shopping
The second floor features clothing stores, electronics shops, accessory boutiques, and toy stores.

Third Floor

Conference Hall
Location: Third floor, next to the exhibition center
Capacity: Seats up to 200 people
Facilities: Audio-visual equipment, Wi-Fi, catering services available
Booking: Contact mall management at info@centralcitymall.com

Exhibition Center
A flexible space for art exhibitions, product launches, and community events. Adjacent to the conference hall.

Entertainment Zone
Movie theater with multiple screens
Arcade and gaming center
Kids' play area with supervised activities

Washrooms
Washrooms are located near the entertainment zone on the third floor.

Transportation and Access

Subway Connection
From the ground floor, walk toward the supermarket. A clearly marked hallway leads directly to the subway entrance. The subway station connects to major city routes.

Parking
Multi-level parking is available with spaces for over 500 vehicles. Accessible parking spots are near all elevator entrances.

Bus Access
Bus stops are located at the front and rear entrances of the mall. Multiple bus routes serve the mall throughout the day.

Taxi and Ride-Share
Designated pick-up and drop-off zones are at the main entrance for taxis and ride-share services.

Facilities and Services

Information Desk
Located at the main entrance on the ground floor. Staff can provide directions, answer questions, and assist with inquiries.

ATM Machines
ATMs are available on every floor near the escalators.

Free Wi-Fi
The mall offers free Wi-Fi throughout all floors. Connect to "CentralCityMall_Free" network.

Lost and Found
Located at the security office on the ground floor. Contact security staff if you lose or find items.

Accessibility
Wheelchair ramps and elevators provide access to all floors. Accessible washrooms are available on every floor. Reserved parking is near all entrances.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the coffee shop?
The coffee shop, Coffee Breeze Café, is on the ground floor to the left of the main entrance.

Where can I get Chinese dishes?
On the second floor at the food court—look for Dragon Wok Restaurant.

Where can I find Sri Lankan food?
Also on the second floor, at Ceylon Spice Kitchen, near the main food court area.

Where is the washroom?
Washrooms are on every floor near the escalators. The main one is on the second floor.

Where is the conference hall?
It is on the third floor, next to the exhibition center.

How do I go to the subway?
From the ground floor, walk toward the supermarket. A marked hallway leads directly to the subway entrance.

What are the mall hours?
The mall is open from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM daily. Individual stores and restaurants may have different hours.

Is there parking available?
Yes, multi-level parking with over 500 spaces is available. Parking fees apply.

Does the mall have Wi-Fi?
Yes, free Wi-Fi is available throughout the mall. Connect to "CentralCityMall_Free."

Can I book the conference hall?
Yes, contact mall management at info@centralcitymall.com or call +94 11 234 5678 to book.

Are there facilities for people with disabilities?
Yes, wheelchair ramps, elevators, accessible washrooms, and reserved parking are available on all floors.

Central City Mall regularly hosts seasonal sales, cultural festivals, and community events. Check the information desk or website for upcoming events and special promotions.
